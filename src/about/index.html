Yulia Katan is a Berlin-based artist from Odessa, Ukraine. She creates drawings, paintings, objects inspired by European medieval culture, nature and dream symbolism. She primarely focuses on figurative drawing and surreal imagery.

„I explore the world around and myself through feelings, transforming them into symbols. I arrange my reality with my artwork. It is a way of communicating with people. There are subtleties of perception, vague sensations, events of inner life that cannot be put into words. The language of symbols is mostly universal.“
