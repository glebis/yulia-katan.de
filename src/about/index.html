Yulia Katan is a Berlin-based artist from Odessa, Ukraine. She creates drawings, paintings, objects inspired by European medieval culture, nature and dream symbolism. She primarely focuses on figurative drawing and surreal imagery.

„I explore myself and the world around through feelings, transforming them into symbols. I arrange my reality with my artwork. It is a way of communicating with people. There are subtleties of perception, vague sensations, events of the inner life which cannot be described in words, while the language of symbols - mostly universal.“
